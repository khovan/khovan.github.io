About Kate

Kate Hovan is a student at the University of Miami who is majoring in print journalism and minoring in history. She participated in the "Fordham Nightly News" program as an anchor, reporter and technical assistant during her freshman year at Fordham University.

After transferring to UM, she served as an associate producer, anchor and reporter for the award-winning campus newscast, "NewsVision," and wrote for The Miami Hurricane. She has experience in both print and broadcast journalism, but she is seeking a career in production, editing, directing and/or writing for broadcast. Over the course of her college career, she developed an understanding in the following areas:

Television news producing, web design, writing for print and broadcast journalism, fundamentals of newsgathering, freedom of expression, understanding media and content, electronic media production, mobile journalism, covering the arts, documentary photography and communication law and policy.
